# PayFlow Labs

**Website:** [https://payflowlabs.vercel.app](https://payflowlabs.vercel.app)
**Date:** February 15, 2026

## What is it?

An intuitive UX Simulator for Payment flows.

## Who is it for?

1. **UX Designers:** To refer to payment flow best practices.
2. **Startups:** To explore payment options for their applications.

## Why was it created?

1. There are many payment gateways besides credit cards, such as points, crypto, and QR. This app helps to connect the pain point of understanding multiple types of payment methods used by these gateways.
2. It helps startups explore payment gateways and methods that they may not have come across and test them with users before implementing them on their own apps.

## Features

1. **Examples of real-world payment apps:** Shopify, Amazon, Airbnb, GrabPay, Shopee, and more.
2. **Examples of payment gateways:** Credit card, Biometric, FPX, QRPay, Crypto, Reward Points, and Voucher Code.
3. **Examples of edge cases:** Connectivity issues, Refunds, Low funds, and Cancellations.
4. **Mastering Payment UX Design:** A condensed eBook covering mechanics, ideal flows, and UI best practices.

## Tech Specs

1. Web application built using **Next.js**
2. Styling with **Shadcn UI** and **TailwindCSS**
3. Built using **Claude Sonnet 4.5**
4. Integrates **Google OAuth** for Login
5. **Supabase** as the core database
6. Deployed on **GitHub** and **Vercel**
7. Built in **Antigravity IDE**
